About

A game inspired by Tamagotchi mechanics, which revolve around taking care of a pet. The pet in our game is going to be a pink fairy armadillo, and there will be a page introducing the armadillo and highlighting conservation efforts towards it.

How to Play with your Pet:

You need to perform actions with your pet in order to keep it happy and healthy. If you do not keep it happy and healthy your pets health bar will go down and he may become sick or leave you for a more caring owner!

Your pet will be able to tell if your pet is happy or sad as his image will change depending on how well you are looking after him.
